Using Escape Characters to display reserved characters

Less-than bracket: <

Greater-than bracket: >

Ampersand: &

Quotation Mark: "

Cent sign: ¢

Pound Sign: £

Yen: ¥

Euro sign: €

Copyright: ©

Registered Trademark: ®

Trademark: ™

Left single quote: ‘

Right single quote: ’

Left double quote: “

Right double quote: ”

Multiplication sign: ×

Division sign: ÷
