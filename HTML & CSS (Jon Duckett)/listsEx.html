Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favourite foods. Here's a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbps butter
  • 2tbps cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 10 seconds (as if you are making an omlette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served.
